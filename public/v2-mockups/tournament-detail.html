How It Works — Americano Format
1
Check-in & warm-up (9:00–9:20)
All registered players check in at the club desk. Court assignments posted at 9:00. 20-minute warm-up period before round 1.
2
Americano rounds (9:20–12:30)
Partners and opponents rotate every round. Each round is played to 16 points. Every point is tracked — your personal score is the total points you win across all rounds, regardless of partner. You play with and against everyone.
3
Live standings update in real time
Scores submitted on the Padel Z app after each round. The leaderboard on your phone updates as the tournament progresses — no waiting for a scoreboard.
4
Final standings & season points (12:30–13:00)
Top 3 announced. Season leaderboard updated automatically — all participants earn points. Results published on your player profile.
